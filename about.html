HELLO!

Sara Freidoony is an international illustrator, working remotely with clients around the world.

As an artist devoted to children's literature, she creates book illustrations and digital art that captures the whimsical essence of childhood. She believes there's something magical about seeing the world from a child's perspective—their movements, personalities, and unique perceptions inspire every piece she creates.

Since childhood, Sara has dreamed of building imaginative worlds filled with joy, emotion, and unexpected wonders. Today, her mission is to invite young readers into magical, fictional landscapes where anything is possible. She has illustrated two published books with Mehrsa Publishing and Neshane Amooz Publishing and contributed artwork to international platforms such as Storytime Magazine and Atlasia Kids. She is currently illustrating Problems’ Problems for Asala Publishers and I Love You Dad for Al-Noor Educational Publishing, and previously completed a year-long monthly illustration series for Poopak Magazine.

In her spare time, Sara finds joy in playing classical piano and losing herself in the world of plays and literature.
