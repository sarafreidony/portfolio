HELLO!

Sara Freidoony is an international illustrator based in Iran, working remotely with clients around the world.

As an artist devoted to children's literature, she creates book illustrations and digital art that captures the whimsical essence of childhood. She believes there's something magical about seeing the world from a child's perspectiveâ€”their movements, personalities, and unique perceptions inspire every piece she creates.

Since childhood, Sara has dreamed of building imaginative worlds filled with joy, emotion, and unexpected wonders. Today, her mission is to invite young readers on journeys through magical, fictional landscapes where anything is possible. She graduated with distinction in Painting from the University of Tehran and ranked 3rd among 100,000 contenders in the national entrance exam. Her work has appeared in top Iranian publications including Mehrsa Publishing, Poopak Magazine, and Neshane Amooz Publishing.

In her spare time, Sara finds joy in playing classical piano and losing herself in the world of plays and literature.
