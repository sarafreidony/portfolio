Hello! I'm Sara Freidoony, an international illustrator and character designer with a passion for crafting imaginative worlds. I specialize in bringing stories to life through art, with a focus on children's perspectives—capturing the magic, wonder, and softness they see in the world around them. My illustrations blend gentle colors and expressive characters to evoke a sense of light and warmth.

With experience collaborating with publishers and magazines, I've honed my craft to create visuals that resonate and inspire. I'm driven by a love for storytelling and am always excited to explore new creative opportunities. Before pursuing my career in illustration, I graduated from the University of Tehran, one of the top art institutions in my home country, where I ranked third in my field and third in the national university entrance exam—a foundation that fuels my dedication to excellence.

I'm eager to connect with clients and collaborators who share a love for whimsical, heartfelt design. Let's create something magical together!
